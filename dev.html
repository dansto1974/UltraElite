Getting Started
  • Launch from Lave Station, steer with W/A/S/D and Q/E, and use +/- for speed.
  • Buy cargo in Market, pick a nearby system on the chart, then use Jump when clear.
  • Keep an eye on shields, energy, laser heat, cabin temperature and fuel.
  • Dock by flying slowly through the station slot when it is aligned, or use Auto Dock if fitted.
  • Open the right-hand panel with the arrow tab and choose Help for full controls and the update log.
